Who We Are

Welcome!

Hello! We are Hugs to Elders — a student-created and student-led project made to bring warmth and joy to the elderly in our community through creating care packages for seniors and volunteering.

Alongside this, we hope to spread more awareness about issues affecting the elderly community through informative posts and monthly newsletters.

About Us

Our Mission

We believe every senior deserves to feel seen, valued, and loved.

Care Packages

We assemble and deliver thoughtful care packages filled with hygiene items, comfort goodies, and handwritten notes to seniors in need.

Volunteering

From bracelet-making to origami sessions, we organize events that connect students with seniors and create lasting intergenerational bonds.

Awareness

Through social media, newsletters, and community outreach, we educate others about the challenges facing the elderly community.

Letters from H2E

Handwritten letters and cards bring personal warmth and connection directly to seniors who may feel isolated or alone.

Our Story

In September, we delivered our first-ever batch of 50+ care packages to UCSF senior patients. Each package contained hygiene items, comfort goodies, and a handwritten note from our team. We are grateful to the Acute Care for Elders Team for helping us deliver these packages and for featuring our work in UCSF GeriNews.

What started as a small student initiative has grown into a movement of compassion — bake sales, letter-making events, and volunteer days that bring our community together for a cause we deeply believe in.
